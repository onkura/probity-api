
# Scope & Non-Goals

Probity does not:

- enforce policy
- block actions
- judge correctness
- replace logging
- guarantee safety

It records decision evidence only.

## Legal & Discovery Note

Probity is an evidence standard, not legal advice. Records may become discoverable under litigation or regulatory requests. Organizations SHOULD coordinate with legal counsel to define retention, redaction, and privilege policies before enabling raw-capture modes. Probity does not provide legal privilege protection.
