
# Reproducibility Model
Probity preserves interpretability, not determinism.

Replaying a record reconstructs context, not behavior.

Different outcomes later do not invalidate the record.

### Interpretation Stability

A valid Probity record MUST produce materially equivalent conclusions when interpreted by independent reviewers following this specification.

The record does not guarantee reproducibility of the decision process.
The record guarantees stability of interpretation of the committed action and its basis.
